# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors and participants in the Access Review System community. We believe that diversity and inclusion strengthen our project and make it better.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- **Respect**: Treating all individuals with kindness and respect
- **Inclusivity**: Welcoming people from all backgrounds and experience levels
- **Collaboration**: Working together constructively and openly
- **Constructive Feedback**: Providing and receiving feedback in a helpful manner
- **Accountability**: Taking responsibility for our actions
- **Professionalism**: Using appropriate language and avoiding disruptive behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or abuse of any kind
- Offensive comments related to gender, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion, or age
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Intimidation or threats
- Posting private information without consent
- Spam or trolling
- Disruption of discussions or community spaces
- Any other conduct that is harmful or inappropriate

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project spaces (GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Project events and meetings
- Informal gatherings related to the project
- Any space where community members are representing the project

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it by:

1. **Direct Message**: Contact a project maintainer directly
2. **Email**: moditha@example.com (or appropriate contact)
3. **GitHub**: Report through GitHub's reporting tools

**When reporting, please include:**

- Description of the behavior
- Who was involved
- When and where it occurred
- Context and any evidence
- Any additional information

### Confidentiality

All reports will be handled with discretion. We will:

- Take all reports seriously
- Investigate promptly and fairly
- Maintain confidentiality of reporters
- Not tolerate retaliation against reporters

### Response

Upon receiving a report, the team will:

1. **Review** the report and gather information
2. **Investigate** the situation fairly
3. **Determine** appropriate action
4. **Communicate** outcomes to involved parties
5. **Follow Up** as needed

Possible actions include:

- Warnings and requests for behavioral changes
- Temporary or permanent bans from community spaces
- Removal of content
- Escalation to appropriate authorities
